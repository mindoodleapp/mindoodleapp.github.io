Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Mindoodle ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Mindoodle application.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Drawing Images: Photos of drawings uploaded by parents or teachers for analysis purposes.
  • Account Information: Email address and name for account creation and management.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics to improve app performance and user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information exclusively for:

  • Providing AI-powered drawing analysis and developmental insights.
  • Improving our analysis algorithms and app functionality.
  • Communicating with you about your account and app updates.

4. Children's Privacy

Mindoodle is designed to be used by parents and teachers on behalf of children. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. All interactions with the app are intended to be performed by adult users (parents, guardians, or teachers).

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud infrastructure). We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Drawing images are processed solely for analysis purposes and are not shared with any external entities.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact us at kenan.baylan4654@gmail.com.

8. In-App Purchases

Mindoodle offers optional premium features through in-app purchases. These transactions are processed securely through Apple's App Store payment system.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes through the app or via email.
